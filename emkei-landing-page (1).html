About EMKEI Innovations

EMKEI Innovations is a Kenyan-based Life Sciences Process Solutions organization committed to enabling sustainable biotechnology growth across Africa. We provide end-to-end support for both mission-aligned NGOs and private sector innovators seeking to establish or expand life sciences operations on the continent.

At the core of our work is a belief that Africa's health and economic resilience depends on locally driven biotech ecosystems. EMKEI is here to help build them—through thought leadership, strategic execution, and support for local manufacturing.
